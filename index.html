Singer/Composer Kenji Navarrete Yoshida, known as Kenji Kid, was born in Caracas, Venezuela on November 21th, 1996. Kenji began his musical career at the age of 12 as a guitarist, venturing into genres such as metal & progressive rock with his father, a drummer who was present on the rock scene in the 90's in Caracas. Recently, Kenji is experimenting with genres like hip-hop, metal, R&B, pop, rock; looking for syncretism between genres. He is currently running this project at Buenos Aires, Argentina.
